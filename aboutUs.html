What is Errand Folks?

Errand Folks is an online web application, designed to make day to day life easier for people with not enough time, and more rewarding for those with too much time. On Errand Folks, you are able to create a User Account, which is able to make, as well as reply to listings. Listings are posts on our forum which either request or offer services. Users are able to create listings for all kinds of different services ranging from food, to sports. Any user who comes across a listing is able to reply to the post in order to offer advice, ask further questions regarding the listing, or comment whatever comes across their mind. It is our hope, that through Errand Folks, no matter what issue you have, you'll find someone who can help.
